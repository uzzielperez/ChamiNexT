## 2. What SIG / systematic-fund HMs are testing (HM round ≠ puzzle round)

They've likely already seen your technical screen. The HM round is:

1. **Intellectual honesty** — real mistakes, real process fixes  
2. **Adversarial reasoning** — defend claims under pushback (sample size, costs, leakage)  
3. **Production instinct** — backtest → live → kill criteria  
4. **Team fit** — disagree well, commit after decisions  
5. **Curiosity about *their* research culture** — not generic "what's culture like"

**Vocabulary that lands:** point-in-time, as-of join, look-ahead, survivorship, multiple testing, capacity, participation rate, alpha decay, kill switch, paper trading.

**Avoid:** LeetCode war stories, buzzword ML without eval discipline, "we had Sharpe 3" without cost model.

## 3. Your 90-second story (candidate mode)

Structure:

> **Context** — physics/ML background, research that touched real data pipelines and validation  
> **Proof** — one example where you *caught* a bad signal or fixed a methodology mistake before it cost money/time  
> **Why quant** — you want adversarial, evidence-driven environments (SIG/Theorem fit)  
> **Why now** — specific role/team angle if you know it  

**HM question to prepare:** *"Tell me about a time you were wrong in research."*  
Use a real story: metric that broke → how you found out → process artifact (test, monitor, review gate).

Practice on platform: **`quant-hm-behavioral`**, **`quant-hm-culture`**.

## 6. Three ways they can help (pick one ask)

### Option A — Design partner (no cash, lowest friction)

**Ask:** *"Would you spend 30 minutes critiquing our quant rubric? If it's useful, run one mock loop on your next intern/research hire."*

**You give:** Free 60-day pilot on one role, custom quant problem pack, white-glove setup.

**They get:** Better signal on hires; influence on rubric; early access.

---

### Option B — Referral / champion (social proof)

**Ask:** *"If you know one fund or systematic shop struggling with hiring signal, I'll give your referral a free Interview Season for their candidates and a pilot for the firm."*

**You give:** Referral code, co-branded quant prep path, LinkedIn testimonial if it works.

**Economics for them:** Zero cost; reputational upside as someone fixing hiring.
